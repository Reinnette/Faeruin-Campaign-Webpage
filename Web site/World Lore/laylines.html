Laylines

There are 13 Laylines within the world. Each reprecents a different element and is controlled by a different Arch Fae.

There are 12 Laylines that make up a circle that all feed into the 13th that lies in the center of the map known as the Great Old Tree

The Lalines are as follows:

Light:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by the White Witch. This court is govern over a standard of no crime can be commited. Even if a person attempts to commit on its as if some force makes it not happen. This strange power of the region also seems to benifit healing based effects allowing them to have the maximum effect, also any spell maid to cause hard has no effect here and if one is attemtped to be casted its as if its instantly counterspelled.

Ice:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by Wizard of Oz. This court also known as the court of the frozen song is a cold but magical land. It is always assumed that plants do not grow here but in reality many plants that appear frozen rezide here moving and growing as lush as any other.

Force:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by ?????. A cournt were the laws of gravity do not apply in the normal means. Mountains float upside down, held down by chains to no let them float into the sky. While possibly not even a leap away the gravity is so high even a dragon would have to walk here.

Physic:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by Queen Candy.

Wind:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by Tinker Bell.

Posion:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Rumblestilskin.

Dark:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Maleficent. A region of constant midnight where the beauty of Lunestra shines down from above. In this land all things that Human's think of as the dark and the spooky reside and live together in harmony. Loving visitors the people of this court will welcome any and all with open arms and a showing thatll mesmerize the eye.

Fire:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Queen of Hearts. A region of uncontroled flame like a furnace running at all times!

Lightning:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Wicked Witch. A region of spark and thunder were balls of static float through the air and pillars of static can be seen for miles. Though seeminly a dangerous land many come to find it their home living my the rules of do not anger the forest and the forest shale not bring anger onto you.

Water:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Salcius(Shadow Garden). Salcius controlls a region of Sadows along with the element of Water, his region borders the ocean city of Moon Shale Iles and the Chery blossom Delight. It can largly be described as a light hearted and kind land to those that are welcome. Though to those that are unwelcome or find them selfs wandering at night can see strange shadows lurking around the area. The reach of their effects even stretches as far south as the Ghostly Hulls. Some pirates and other ships have gone missing with rare survivors claiming several hands rose from the sea and drug them under.

Earth:

A Uneelie Fae court rulled over by Eins Ull Goan. A land of ever changing from the spikes and forests to the mountains that stretch into the sky. This land is said to be alive in all sense of the word, being able to change the entire region in an instant for better or for worse.

Air:

A Seelie Fae court rulled over by King Arthur.
