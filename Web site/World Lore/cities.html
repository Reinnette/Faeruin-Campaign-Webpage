Faeruin

Faeruin is a city built around the roots of a giant tree. Constructed in sections of rings that encompass the base of it and for its main gates has two giant boulders.

Oana

Oana is a peaceful town full of party, fun and laughter though some may come to know it as the city of ammusment as it is home to the biggest carnival known to the region. It is said that even Mr. Witch and Mr. Light do visit here for some fun and ammusment, as theirs always somthing new going on. One of the bigger attractions is a bottomless hole that no one know the origin of that produces a slight anti-gravity field allowing those inside to float in the air several feet. The city as a whole is run from the laughter of its people and quite literaly changes based on the mood. This is due to everything from the color of the tents and rides to the music and charm in the air is all powered from this effect.

Many people can be found hear for one reason or another, weather they came on purpose or were drawn by its charms. Even including the Witchlight Carnival from time to time, as it works with the cities attractions swapping them in and out durring each visit.

Four districts make up this city, each with a different feel. The Water Park, Ammusment Park, and Theme Park make up a circle with the Circus in the center. Both the inner circle and the outer ring rotate in line with the sun though were as the outer circle rotates clock wise the inner circle rotates clockwise. Due to it following the fun the outer circler will be at the following locations and times: 12am Water Park will be towards Faeruin, 6am Water Park will be towards the cherry blossom delight, 12pm Water Park will be alligned with the docks, 6pm the water park will be facing the crystal peaks

Water Park: Wave Pool, Water Slide, Tube Slide, Water Bowl, Lasy River, Log Rides, Rune Squirt guns(casts create water), water balloons, Battle Ships(mending for repair, first team to sink the other ships win)

Amusement park: Horse Ride, Swings, Spinny rides, Wodden Roller Coaster, Spinny Roller Coaster, Gemini Coaster, Bumber Cars, Farris Wheel, Gondola

Theme Park: Dunk tank, strength test, ring the bottle, future telling, bottle shooting, maze, haunted house, hall of mirrors,

Circus: Horse Racing, Jousting, Circus Tent,

Monster Inc Laughter encounter were the fae are visiting the other realms to take children's laughter to fuel their amusement center

Daliah

Once known as the city of never winter that was once transported away from the material plane to protect itself from a great danger. Known for its enginering in water clocks, gardens, fruits, and other advancments it was sought after and thrived for many year.

As time went on and decades passed in a blur it was renamed to Daliah by the locals of the new land. Soon people from all over wanted to see this new place catching the eye of the Dwarfs and those with a will to tinker.

Daliah nexted within the crystal peak mountains known for its specticals as the sun moves across the sky and hits different holes in the mointain tops. This hits different crystals and shines colored light to tye city bellow. Due to the way the lights hit the city, it has split itself into several districts. Blue, Red, Yellow, Black, Green. Blue Lit District:The district of magic. With a focus on runes and spells. Red Lit District: The district of combat. Those who are knights or proud warries are drawn to this district. Many late night tavern brawles can be found here. Yellow Lit District: The research district. Many artificers are drawn here for technical research. The auto crossbows and spell guns were made here. Black Lit District: At the heart of the city and also known as the rainbow district. This is the buisiest part of the city due to the shopping district, trade shops and more being here. Green Lit District: The floral district. This district is known as the farming district. Being lush and vibrant

At the cities heart is the black light district. This is the buisiest part of the city getting its name from the giant crystal that reflects the incomming sunlight bathing the region in a multitude of shimmering colors. Though all around can find people tinkering with the old and creating the new. Everything from the lamps in the streats, to crossbows that fire multiple times, to mechanical humans taking thier first breaths.

City of Clifs

The City of Clifs gets its name from being up in the clif sids of several steep mountains known as the Drowning Peaks. This city though rests roughly 100ft off the ground and is comprised of a race known as the Catfae[Cuh-fae] and some harppies. They guard a dungeon said to hold a great mystery but refuse to let just anyone enter their domain. As this city does sit on the sides of clifs its residents have leared to work with it making small bridges between the cliff faces as well as houses that hang from the underside of rock. Though food and water may seem hard to come by here they have learned to harvest gallons of rain watter that they use for their crops, cattle and daily needs.

A city built onto the side of the cliffs. Home to the Catfae, Harpies, and some Aracrocra. This city is largely made up of connected structures with very little bridges spanning from one to the next. Sometimes, there is 20ft of height difference and / or separation between each. Each structure is balanced with the rocks and brick like support columns while the houses are made from more branches, mud, and some paper. The city is known for its wide array of milks that the catfae have come to love. High within the city within the chiefs office lies a large old door. Inside is a dungeon that protects a great secret.
