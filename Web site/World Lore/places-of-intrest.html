These mountains are not to be Traversed lightly, feared by many for being the home to a vast population of dragons Each mountain peak holds a different type of dragon.

Dwelling of Spirits

The dwelling of Spirits is as old as the Laylines themselfs. Once attempted to be corrupted by the Hand of Ragnarok this place holds the Spirit Queen who granted select people with the gift to summon a familiar. This place can be reconized from a single great tree in the middle of a clearing. Those the Queen of spirits allow are able to enter the tree into her dimension.

Inside the queen of Spirits dimension is like a Labyrinth with each twist and turn confusing the senses. Once at the center you will be greeted by the Queen of Spirits. Within this place she is able to call forth any willing spirit into existance.
