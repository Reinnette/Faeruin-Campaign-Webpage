As the party explores the caves they will find hints at to what caused this such as specific monsters that eat crystals as well as smaller worms in the area.

Lady of the reflecting waters

There are reports of a monster that is turning men into pigs over by the reflecting waters. The players should investigate about what people have heard and seen as if they get a high role they will learn that one of the townsfolk are seen heading to the lake each evning. If the players choose to investigate the npc they will find out her side of the story. She explains that she has lived in this city for quite a while but has a duty to protect the lake. People of the town keep coming looking for the lost city of Atlantis and or to capture her younger sisters to sell them off into slavery. Once the players go to the lake they will see armed warriors fighting a siren with to young ones behind her. The party can choose what to do weather to help fight against her or weather to help her. If they help her after defeating or calling off the towns folk they will get her blessing allowing the party to indeed enter the thought to be lost city of Atlantis.

The non-Fae Fae

Raining Cats and Spirits

The party gets word that in a neighboring city it has had spirit animals falling out of the sky seemly randomly. They would like the party to investigate this as it is starting to cause damage to the buildings and harm to the people. The party is directed to go to the local guild hall for more information. The guild will tell them that many people have submitted missing spirit animals requests but none have ever been found and they always disapear at night. As the players investigate one of the spirit animals is about to fall on them (DC12 Dex save). After watching the sky the party can see that birds are randomly turning into animals and falling. The party can either talk to one of the animals, try to devise a trap to see where the animals are going, of if one of the players roll an perception 18 can see a shadow on the roof tops. Once found out the party can head in the direction where the reports are comming from where they will find a cultist that is taking spirit animals and experimenting on them. His goals are to see what the limits are to such where once enganged he will toss out a few spirit animals as pawns to fight the party. The cultist himself isnt to strong but where he is strong is in numbers due to commanding/controlling so many different spirits.

The Whishing Wells

Ever wonder where whish's from a whishing well come from. Well one of the local wells has stopped producing whishes and the party is asked to figure out why. Apon inspection of the whishing well and throwing a coin in it can be seen that the coin magically disapears just as it normally would do, but rather than granting a small and simple whish nothing happens. After some investigation or if the party needs help have a stranger bumb into they and see if either they or some other object falls into the well. At which point the players should notice that not only coins can go into the well. Once all players enter they are meet with a new world and a person that is very unhappy to see them as they are not allowed to be there. But none the less they welcome the party to the whishing world where many giene's reside and recieve that is then turned into a small and simple whish. Almost all of the giene's love this though some do try to escape tho its said they live lives within containers making wishes for random people without payment. There is an issue though as pixies have found their way in and are stealing the coins before the geines and get them. The party is told that the pixies always take the coins back through to a specific well
